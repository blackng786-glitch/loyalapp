Which one is right for you?

The honest answer — each platform has a best-fit customer.

Choose StoreHub if…

You also need a full POS system with LHDN e-Invoicing, want loyalty baked into checkout, and a ~RM3,300+/year budget (POS + loyalty + SMS add-on) works for you.

Choose Stamp Me if…

You want a long-established international brand with a simple punch-card model, and you're confident your customers will download a separate app to collect stamps.

Choose Choppkar if…

You run a cafe, bar, KTV, or restaurant and want stamp cards, member tiers, bottle keep, vouchers, and referrals — without buying a POS, without forcing an app on customers, starting free and topping out at RM49/month.
